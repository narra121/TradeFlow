## Notes on the Email

### Why this works:
1. **Short** — under 150 words, scannable in 30 seconds
2. **Opens with a genuine compliment** about their article (not fake flattery)
3. **Leads with what's different** — free tier + AI extraction (their readers care about this)
4. **Offers free premium access** — gives them a reason to respond
5. **No pressure** — "if it doesn't make the cut, totally fine"
6. **Clear CTA** — one simple question: "Would you be interested?"

### Personalization required for each email:
- [ ] Replace [First Name] with the actual author's name
- [ ] Replace [article title](link) with their specific article
- [ ] Replace [TraderSync/TradeZella/etc.] with journals they actually reviewed
- [ ] Read their article first — mention something specific you liked

### What NOT to do:
- Don't send the same email to all 12 bloggers word-for-word
- Don't ask them to "add you to their list" — ask if they'd check you out
- Don't attach anything — keep it plain text
- Don't follow up more than twice (Day 1, Day 7)
- Don't offer money for a review — it violates Google's guidelines
